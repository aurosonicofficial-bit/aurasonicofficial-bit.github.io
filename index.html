WHO WE ARE

AURA SONIC OFFICIAL

Aura Sonic Official is a United Kingdom-based independent record label dedicated to representing visionary artists across a wide range of genres — from atmospheric rock and soul pop to Turkish folk music and multilingual pop.

We believe in authentic storytelling through music. Each artist on our roster brings a unique voice to the world stage, distributed globally through Mitas.

Founded in 2026, Aura Sonic Official is home to both established voices and rising talents, united by a shared commitment to artistic integrity and musical excellence.
